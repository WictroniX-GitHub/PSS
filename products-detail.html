MV Switchgear

Description


MV switchgear is the heart of medium voltage power distribution. It houses circuit breakers, switches, and other control devices to protect, monitor, and distribute electrical power.

Features


    

Compact, modular designs for space optimization.


    

Advanced switchgear technology for high reliability and operational safety.


    

Remote monitoring and control capabilities.


    

Arc-flash mitigation systems.


Benefits


    

Enhanced power system reliability and minimized downtime.


    

Increased operational safety for personnel.


    

Improved energy efficiency and reduced operational costs.


    

Scalability to meet evolving power demands.


EHV Circuit breaker

Description


EHV circuit breakers are critical for the protection and control of high voltage power grids. They interrupt dangerous fault currents and allow for safe isolation of grid sections.

Features


    

Extremely high current interruption capability.


    

Fast operating mechanisms.


    

Sulfur Hexafluoride (SF6) gas-insulated or air-insulated designs.


    

Advanced monitoring and diagnostics.


Benefits


    

Maintains power grid stability and reliability.


    

Protects valuable grid assets from damage during faults.


    

Facilitates safe maintenance and repair of EHV equipment.


    

Long operational lifespan with high reliability.


Isolator/ Disconnector

Description


Isolators and disconnectors create a safe physical break in electrical circuits, ensuring zero energy flow during maintenance or expansion.

Features


    

Robust mechanical designs for reliable operation.


    

High-quality insulating materials.


    

Clear indication of open/closed position.


    

Interlocking systems to prevent operation under load.


Benefits


    

Essential for workplace safety during electrical work.


    

Durable design for long life and minimal maintenance.


    

Available in a range of voltage classes to suit different applications.



LT SwitchGear

Description


LT switchgear controls and distributes low voltage power for everyday applications.

Features


    

Modular and compact designs.


    

Built-in protection devices (circuit breakers, fuses).


    

Metering and monitoring options.


Benefits


    

Safe and reliable power distribution.


    

Easy to install and maintain.


    

Wide range of configurations to meet various needs.
